# Boot Sequence Update — Tiny Orb Wake Only

This update removes the rejected old activation symbol direction entirely.

## New boot lock

- Start on a black screen.
- Show only a tiny centered Still Salt orb wake target.
- The user can click the tiny orb or speak the wake phrase to wake the AI.
- The wake phrase is voice-only and must not be written on the app screen.
- The camera follows the small orb through a blue/orange dashboard ecosystem hallway/tunnel.
- Flying panels, tasks, updates, metrics, maps, product scan fragments, and security/system data pass by.
- The orb and dashboard approach together.
- The orb turns and locks into place like a key.
- The dashboard powers on like an Iron Man suit sequence.
- Then the AI begins the spoken welcome: “Welcome back, sir. Thank you for saving our world again. What would we do without you?”
- Daily briefing begins after the welcome.

## Why this change exists

This removes color-scheme confusion and prevents the next builder from designing around the rejected activation symbol.
