# Still Salt Command Center — Design Lock

## Locked visual direction
The app must feel like a premium futuristic video-game AI command center. It is not a normal SaaS dashboard.

## Colors
- Main: electric neon blue / cyan blue.
- Accent: hot neon orange.
- Background: deep black tech void.
- Do not use the rejected old activation symbol direction.
- Boot activation must begin with a tiny centered Still Salt orb only.
- The spoken wake phrase may be used by the user, but the wake phrase must not be written anywhere on the app screen.

## Dashboard idle
- Top center: STILL SALT.
- Subtitle: COMMAND CENTER.
- Center: exactly two-layer Still Salt orb.
- Left/right panels: futuristic system/data/AI/product panels.
- Bottom buttons: TikTok SCAN, Mute/INCOGNITO, Results/LAUNCHPAD, Amazon SCAN.
- Blue electricity moves through borders.
- Orange accents pulse.
- Orb spins and breathes.

## Two-layer orb rule
Exactly two visible orb layers:
1. Inner electric blue SS Still Salt orb.
2. Outer blue/orange electric reactor ring.

Do not make one orb. Do not make three orbs. Do not remove the outer ring. Do not add random text inside the orb.

## Talking mode
- Side panels slide/fade away.
- Orb grows large and comes forward.
- Two-layer orb remains intact.
- A small transparent electric-blue chat box appears under the orb.
- Chat box is small, about 1/8 screen impact.
- Blue text types inside.
- Orb pulses/spins/breathes to voice.
- Dashboard returns after speaking.

## Results / Launchpad mode
- Center results/launchpad panel opens.
- Orb docks to the side.
- Orb remains fully active: listens, responds, speaks unless muted, pulses, spins, and communicates.
- Minimize returns panel down and orb back to center.

## Incognito / Silent mode
- Triggered by Mute.
- Voice disabled.
- Orb dims/offline.
- Secure silent chat appears.
- Blue text only.
- No sound.

## Boot sequence
- Black screen.
- Tiny centered Still Salt orb is the only visible activation target.
- User can click the tiny orb or speak the wake phrase to wake the AI.
- The wake phrase is spoken only; it must not be displayed as written text.
- 10-second electric blue/orange dashboard ecosystem hallway/tunnel.
- Flying panels/tasks/updates/data/maps/metrics/security/product fragments pass by as the camera follows the orb.
- Orb and dashboard approach through the hallway/tunnel.
- Orb turns and locks into position like a key.
- Dashboard powers on like an Iron Man suit sequence.
- Welcome line: “Welcome back, sir. Thank you for saving our world again. What would we do without you?”
- Daily briefing appears.
- Ends on dashboard idle.

## IMPORTANT — CURRENT UI IS NOT THE DESIGN

This repository is a function-core/wireframe shell. The visible HTML/CSS is intentionally plain and must not be copied as the final design. The final dashboard must be designed from the uploaded locked reference images and the design lock notes only.
